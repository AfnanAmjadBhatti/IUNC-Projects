About

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS is a high-performance sports car built by Porsche. It's known for its 4.0-litre high revving naturally aspirated 525 PS (386 kW) engine.

Performance

With a top speed of 296 km/h and an acceleration from 0 - 100 km/h in 3.2 seconds, the Porsche 911 GT3 RS offers impressive race track performance.

Aerodynamics and Design

The car features a fully automatic adjustment of the front diffuser and rear wing, and an Aero wishbone on the front axle for up to 40 kg more downforce.

Lightweight Construction

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS also boasts of consistent lightweight construction with numerous components made of carbon fibre reinforced plastic (CFRP).
